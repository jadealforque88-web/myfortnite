MY FAVORITE ONLINE GAME

FORTNITE

Introduction

Fortnite is a very popular online video game developed by Epic Games and released in 2017. It is played by millions of players worldwide and is known for its exciting gameplay, colorful graphics, and unique building system.

History of Fortnite

Fortnite started with a mode called Save the World, where players fight zombies. Later, the Battle Royale mode was introduced, and it became extremely popular. Since then, Fortnite has continued to grow with new seasons, updates, and events.

Main Game Modes

Battle Royale

100 players fight on an island. The last player or team standing wins.

Creative Mode

Players can build their own maps and games.

Save the World

Players work together to fight monsters and complete missions.

Building System

Fortnite allows players to build structures using materials like wood, brick, and metal.

  • Wood – fast but weak
  • Brick – balanced
  • Metal – strong but slow

Seasons and Battle Pass

Fortnite is updated in seasons. Each season introduces new skins, map changes, and features.

Cross-Platform Play

Players on PC, PlayStation, Xbox, Nintendo Switch, and mobile can all play together.

Fortnite: 5 Famous Guns

Fortnite is a popular online battle royale game where players fight using different weapons.

1. Pump Shotgun

The Pump Shotgun is a powerful close-range weapon.

2. Assault Rifle (SCAR)

The SCAR is a balanced weapon used for medium-range fights.

3. Heavy Sniper Rifle

The Heavy Sniper Rifle is used for long-range attacks.

4. SMG

The SMG has a fast fire rate and is effective in close combat.

5. MK-7 Assault Rifle

The MK-7 Assault Rifle is known for its fast shooting speed.
