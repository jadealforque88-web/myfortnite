<h2>Introduction</h2>
<p>
Fortnite is a very popular online video game developed by Epic Games and released in 2017.
It is played by millions of players worldwide and is known for its exciting gameplay,
colorful graphics, and unique building system.
</p>

<h2>History of Fortnite</h2>
<p>
Fortnite started with a mode called Save the World, where players fight zombies.
Later, the Battle Royale mode was introduced, and it became extremely popular.
Since then, Fortnite has continued to grow with new seasons, updates, and events.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>Main Game Modes</h2>

<h3>Battle Royale</h3>
<p>100 players fight on an island. The last player or team standing wins.</p>

<h3>Creative Mode</h3>
<p>Players can build their own maps and games.</p>

<h3>Save the World</h3>
<p>Players work together to fight monsters and complete missions.</p>

<h2>Building System</h2>
<p>
Fortnite allows players to build structures using materials like wood, brick, and metal.
This helps in defense, movement, and gaining advantage during fights.
</p>
<ul>
<li>Wood – fast but weak</li>
<li>Brick – balanced</li>
<li>Metal – strong but slow</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>Seasons and Battle Pass</h2>
<p>
Fortnite is updated in seasons. Each season introduces new skins, map changes,
and features. Players can buy a Battle Pass to unlock rewards.
</p>

<h2>Cross-Platform Play</h2>
